    3. 3. "Intended" just means where you want that caption to be at in terms of time. For example, if you want "Caption Text 1" to be at 10 seconds after the video starts, you just input 10 seconds in the "sec" box under "Intended."

    5. 5. Finally, once you are finished (or if you left the time box blank in once or more times, it will assume the time in that box is a zero), press submit!
